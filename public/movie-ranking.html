The Best Movies According to Polaris

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Count to Monte Cristo (2002)

This is one of my favorite movies.
I love it and it simply has everything: drama, romance, danger, intrigue.
I simply can't say enough good things about it.

The Grey (2011)

A compelling survival thriller that transcends typical genre conventions through its exploration of human resilience,
the harsh realities of nature, and the psychological toll of facing death.

Predators (2010)

The film manages to remix some of the elements that made the classic original
so timeless without feeling like a redundant retread.
